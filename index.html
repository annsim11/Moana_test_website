Moana

Voice by Auli'i Cravalho

Auli'i Cravalho was born on November 22, 2000 in Kohala, Hawaii, USA as Chloe Auli'i Cravalho. She is an actress, known for Moana (2016), Wreck-It Ralph (2018) and All Together Now (2020). "Moana is such an amazing character. She's brave, she is so empowered, she knows what she wants and she's not afraid to get it, and I think that's something that I can relate to as well. I just love watching how she goes along in this wonderful movie and grows as a person and helps her culture along the way."
